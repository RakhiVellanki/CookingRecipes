Welcome to the Bogus Pie recipe.

A handcrafted family dish!

Ingredients:

Lemon, Vanilla Extract, Pressurized CO2, Salmon Tears, The Thing You Find in the Corner of Your Microwave, Baking Soda, Baking Powder, Cocoa Powder, and Rosemary

Instructions:

  • Step 1: Carve out pulp from Lemon
  • Step 2: Add 2 cups of Cocoa Powder
  • Step 3: Add the Lemon pulp and whisk until expired
  • Step 4: Add the Baking Soda and whisk for at least a couple seconds
  • Step 5: Put the mixture in the freezer for as long as you'd like, I prefer mine around the half-life of Xenon-124.
  • Step 7: After you take out the mixture, compress the air around you into a liquid, and add it to the batter
  • Step 8: I bet you didn't notice there was no step six
  • Step 9: Put 14 dashes of Vanilla Extract into batter
  • Step X: Add 3 half-cups of Baking Powder to the mixture and whisk
  • Step X.5: Do 5 pushups right now
  • Step y: Microwave for 10^52 seconds, and top off with entire Rosemary Plant and Salmon Tears!

And you're done!

Have fun eating this delicious dish!
