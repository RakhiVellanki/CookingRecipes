Welcome to the Dalohoh's Bar Recipe!

This is a very large chocolate bar, share with friend.

Ingredients:

Scrap Metal, Hints of Battery Acid, Raw Cocoa, and Panko Bread Crumbs

Instructions:

  1. Collect at least 2 tonnes of scrap metal from your local compactor(The more the better)
  2. Melt the scrap metal in a large crucible
  3. Add 16 gallons of Acid to your concoction
  4. Grab a new bowl, and add the Cocoa Beans. 4-8
  5. Deflesh the Cocoa beans and but the raw beans in a mixer.
  6. Pour your Panko into the cocoa flesh and take out the cocoa mix
  7. Let the Acid sit overnight
  8. Mix the acid and cocoa powder until colour changes
  9. Put Mix in the oven for 2 hours at 450 farenheit
  10. Once done, put the cocoa flesh on top of the mix, and you should be finished!
